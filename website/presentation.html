What am I?

As an app

My intended scope is to help you, the parent, connect more with your child and help him break through his obstacles.

User friendly

You can use me to help your child get used to a daily routine for a healthier life and track his actions to make sure he is safe and sound.

I can be his teacher

I will use my games to help your kid connect and learn about the world in a fun interactive way.

I can be his friend

I will remember him to make his bed, flush his teeth and make his homework (everything can be set using the parental app).
